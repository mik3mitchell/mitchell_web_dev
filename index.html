A Little About Me

In 2016, I discovered my true passion in web design and development.  I haven't been able to stop building websites, and learning new skills every since.  So, after eight years as a professional in the the Consumer Packaged Goods industry, I have decided chase my dream.

In late 2016, I started seeking out projects that I could work on, and in February of 2017 I landed my first client.  Currently, I am working as a freelance web developer in the North Dallas market.  Some of the websites I have built include sites for a construction company, online body jewlery vendor, and a cattle ranch in Tennessee.

When I'm not creating websites or learning new web skills and technologies, I enjoy spending time with my wife and four kids, playing guitar, and kicking back with the occasional video game.
